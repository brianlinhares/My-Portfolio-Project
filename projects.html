Have you ever heard of PacMan? Well, he is back! This neat game allows you to bring him to your local browser. You'll be able to generate as many PacMen as your machine can handle and they will all bounce around with a life of their own. Enjoy!

Eye Exercise

When you are passing by a doll and its eyes slowly follow you, it can send a chill down your spine. Imagine if those same eyes are following your cursor! This fun program will summon two eyes onto your browser and they are deadset on tracking your cursor... Wherever it goes!
